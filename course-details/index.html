Course Details

Jolong Park is a all in the one, purpose-built, state-of-the-art equestrian property, which is only a short 30-minute drive from Melbourne. Jolong Park features stables, clubhouse, a 2,000 metre sand training track and a 2,200 meter grass racetrack.

The event will take place on the 2,200 meter grass race track. The race distances will consist of full laps of the track, starting at a single lap for a 2.2km total distance, witha longer event of three laps for a 6.6km distance.

Track Location:

Jolong Park, 88-128 Riverend Rd, Bangholme, Victoria

Transport Options:

Car

Jolong Park is just off the Mornington Peninsula Freeway to the East or Eastlink to the West.
Travel time is 45 minutes from Melbourne CBD is 15 minutes from Frankston.
An on site car park will be provided free of charge.

Train

Chelsea Station is a 3km from Chelsea Train Station on the Frankston Line.
