Event Details

Come join in a 6.6km or 2.2km fun run with a difference.
Run on a turf horse racing track in rural Bangholme in Southside Melbourne.
Choose from two races of either one or three laps of the track.

Bib Collection

Friday afternoon and Saturday morning at Jolong Park.

Event Times

2.2km starts at 8.15am, 6.6km starts at 8.30am. The start/finish line is at the same place on the horse track near the main entrance.

Car Parking

An on site car park will be provided free of charge, available off road and on site from 7.00am.

Food and Drink

Water will be provided at the finish of each 2.2km loop. An assortment of food and drinks will be for sale on the day.

Presentations

Presentation of awards will commence at 10.00am under a large shelter near the finish line.

Prizes:

Prize money of $400 ($200 for the 1st Outright Male and Female in the 6.6km)
Medals and sponsors' prizes for Top 3 in the following categories:
